My professional interests include content writing, copy editing, publishing production, design thinking, and social media strategy.

I currently lead social media operations at Texas Master Composter-Coastal Bend Chapter, community-based program of citizen education and volunteer development to promote cost-effective and eco-responsible yard-care and food waste practices. I also write for the Office of Marketing and Communications at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi and provide writing assistance to writers of all levels across higher education. Previously, I was the managing editor of Windward Review, an annual literary journal that showcases narratives unique to South Texas, the Coastal Bend, and border regions.

Feel free to check out my comprehensive portfolio or connect with me on LinkedIn.
